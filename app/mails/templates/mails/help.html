What is rmd.io?

rmd.io is a simple, usefull and free mail-reminder. In the web interface you can manage mails, you sent to a delay address of rmd.io. You determine a date when you want to get reminded and you'll get your mail sent back then.

First Usage

  1. Register
  2. Download vCard (click on the button in the navigation)
  3. Send your mail to the appropriate address

Delay Adresses

In the vCard are just few of addresses as an example to use. You can use your own delay-addresses (e.g. '1337w@rmd.io', this would delay it 1337 weeks).
Use d for days, w for weeks and m for months.

Anti Spam

For default, the anti-spam-measure is disabled.
If you enable it in your personal settings, you have to download the new delay-addresses. A mail, sended to '1d@rmd.io', will get deleted, so you have to use the new addresses.
You can use this code on every delay-address (e.g. '99m.yourkey@rmd.io').
