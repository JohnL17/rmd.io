What is rmd.io?

Missed an important deadline again? Forgot another due date? Too lazy to create a reminder in one of those unusable reminder-apps?
Forget that stuff, here's rmd.io! The only thing you need to do is, to send a mail to us, and we will remind you automagically on the date you set.

What a wonderful time to be alive.

It's as simple as breathing.
For example: send a mail at '5d@rmd.io' and we'll send you the mail back 5 days later. This also works with 'w' for weeks and 'm' for months.


Anti Spam

For default, the anti-spam-measure is disabled.
If you enable it in your personal settings, you have to download the new delay-addresses. You find the download link in the dropdown in the navbar. Mail sent to '1d@rmd.io', will be deleted, so you have to use the new addresses.
You can use this key on every delay-address (e.g. '99m.yourkey@rmd.io').
