What is it for?

rmd.io reminds you about unanswered mails and helps you to manage delegated tasks.

3d@rmd.io means to remind me again in 3 days.

Of course you can manage your mails
in the web GUI. You're able to change
the due date and time easily by clicking the pen icon.

After the due date expired, rmd.io will
send your mail back to you to remind you about it.
Incidentally, attachments are fully supported.
